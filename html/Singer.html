ABOUT INFO

Awards and specific information

In 1999, he signed a contract with Haidie music, which opened the music road.

In 2004, Jiangnan gained wide attention. In 2006, he took part in the Spring Festival Gala of CCTV and sang "after a thousand years"; In the same year, he released the album "Cao Cao" and held his first personal tour. In 2008, he was the torch bearer of Beijing Olympic Games.

In 2014, he won the best Mandarin male singer award of the 25th Taiwan Golden Melody Award for his album "because of you". In 2016, he won the best Mandarin male singer award and the best composer award in the 27th Taiwan Golden Melody Awards respectively for his album "dialogue with myself" and the song "song not written for anyone".

In 2020, he won the best Chinese male singer award of the 2020 Asian pop music award. In 2021, he was nominated for the best Chinese male singer award of the 32nd Golden Melody Awards for his "survivor as you".
